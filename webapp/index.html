Work Experience

Over the course of my career I have gained a wide range of experience in the information technology industry, including roles in technical support, professional services, and DevOps.

After completing my military service as a support engineer, I transitioned to a role in technical support where I provided expert technical assistance to end users. In this role, I was responsible for resolving technical issues, providing training and guidance to users, and developing and maintaining documentation and knowledge management resources. Through this experience, I refined my skills in technical troubleshooting, problem-solving, and customer service, and developed a deep understanding of the importance of effective communication and collaboration.

From there, I moved into a role in professional services, where I worked with a variety of enterprises and small companies to provide monitoring solutions tailored to their specific needs. In this role, I gained extensive experience in information technology and monitoring, and developed a strong understanding of the challenges facing businesses in this space. I also had the opportunity to work with a variety of stakeholders, including development teams, operations teams, and business executives, and developed strong communication and collaboration skills.

After the Professional service role I have transitioned to a DevOps role, where I have continued to build on my existing skills and experience to support development teams in their efforts to build and deploy software applications.

In this role, I honed my skills in DevOps methodologies and practices while working in an agile environment. I have worked with a variety of tools and technologies, including:

  • Gitlab Registry and Harbor container registry for container image management
  • Git/Gitlab for SCM
  • Keycloak IAM for authentication and authorization
  • Kong API Gateway for API management
  • Prometheus and Alertmanager for monitoring and alerting
  • MariaDB and Postgres for database management
  • httpd for web server management

Throughout my work experience, I have developed a strong skill set in areas such as technical troubleshooting, problem-solving, customer service, and project management. I have also had the opportunity to work with a variety of teams and stakeholders, and have developed strong communication and collaboration skills.

My Projects

I created a basic Golang application utilizing the Go HTTP library to create a web server. In order to enhance its functionality, I scoured the internet for suitable web resources, such as index.html and other critical files, then customized and optimized them to my liking.

To make the application widely available I acquired a domain name (tomerschwartz) through GoDaddy and deployed it to an EC2 instance in AWS under the Free Tier program. Furthermore, I implemented a Jenkins pipeline, leveraging the Poll SCM option to detect any changes in GitHub and initiate the build process automatically.

The pipeline then deploys the resulting Golang application image to Docker Hub.
